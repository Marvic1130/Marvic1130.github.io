Developing an AIoT-LLM integrated system designed for disaster response environments, funded by the **Institute of Information & Communications Technology Planning & Evaluation (IITP)** under the **Leading Generative AI Human Resources Development** program.

The system combines AIoT sensing capabilities with large language models to enable intelligent, real-time decision support in disaster scenarios where connectivity and compute resources are constrained.
